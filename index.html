About

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Imperial College London, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Cong Ling. My research focuses on quantum algorithms for sampling problems, with particular interest in applications to lattice-based cryptography. This website contains my publications, GitHub repositories, talks, and contact information.

Research Interests

  • Quantum algorithms
  • Lattice cryptanalysis
  • Lattice Gaussian sampling
  • Markov chain Monte Carlo methods
